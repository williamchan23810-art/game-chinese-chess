📜 Quick Start & Rules Guide

  • Turn Timers: Watch the clock! Your turn timer is dynamic and updates based on the moves played. Make your move before it reaches zero.
  • No Take-Backs: Clicking a piece highlights destination markers. Once you select a move, you must click the green ✓ Confirm Move button. Once confirmed, the move cannot be undone!
  • Yellow Card Penalty: If your timer reaches 0:00, you receive a Yellow Card (only once per game) and a 1-minute emergency extension. Expiring the timer a second time results in an automatic Red Card forfeit!
  • King Escape & Captures: If you are in Check (announced by a voice warning), you must protect or escape your King. Moving into check or leaving your King in check is blocked. If a King is captured directly, the game ends instantly in a loss!
  • How to Invite Friends (Online P2P): Select the Two Players (Online Invite) mode, copy the Guest Link and share it with your friend along with the 4-digit passcode. Note: Online play requires a secure context (HTTPS / Netlify or localhost) to connect!
